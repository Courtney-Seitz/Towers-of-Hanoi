Towers of Hanoi

Instructions: You many move any disk onto a disk of equal or greater size.
For single-color games, the objective is to move the entire set of disks to some other position.
For multi-color games, the objective is to "split" the disks into their component colors

Use keys 1, 2, and 3 to select and then move to a position

This version is playable and solvable with up to 3 colors
